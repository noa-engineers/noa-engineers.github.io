Services

  • engineering consultation
  • master planning
  • economic and analytical studies and reports
  • investigation of electrical problems and deficiencies
  • preparation of contractual documents for electrical, telecommunications, security and fire alarm construction, including consultation and observation during construction

The firm has been engaged by private firms, inter-professional clients, utility companies, and County, State and Federal agencies.  Projects cover industrial-commercial, institutional, educational, recreational, ecclesiastical, civil-utility and governmental facilities, including utility company, military base and airfield, and naval shore installations.  The firm’s experience extends throughout all facets of electrical engineering involving exterior and interior lighting, high and low voltage power generation, conversion and distribution, fire alarm, security, sound, video, voice/data communications, master clock/program bell, energy management and control systems, motor control, grounding, lightning protection, electromagnetic shielding, surge protection, etc.
